PT Soraya Intercine Films

Since its founding by Ram Soraya in 1983, Soraya Intercine Films has grown into one of the most influential production houses in Indonesia.

Under the leadership of CEO Sunil Soraya, the company continues to produce films that combine artistic excellence with commercial appeal, such as 5 cm, Eiffel... I’m in Love, Tenggelamnya Kapal Van der Wijck, Single, Suzzanna: Bernapas dalam Kubur, and Suzzanna: Malam Jumat Kliwon.

For more than four decades, Soraya Intercine Films has remained a symbol of dedication, professionalism, and innovation in Indonesia’s film industry.
